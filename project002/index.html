Penhaligon’s pries open the apothecary tables of ancient Egypt, but to no avail. The empty-handed journey back from Cairo allows us time to smell the roses. We decide to use the city of Cairo as a muse - Cairo instantly enthrals, yet reveals deeper treasures over time.
